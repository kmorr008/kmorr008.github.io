✦
Pathway Completion Requirements
To be an official pathway completer, students must complete AP Computer Science Principles (concentrator) and AP Computer Science A (capstone). CS Discoveries is an optional entry point with no prerequisites. CS 3/4 is an optional advanced course — students must complete AP CS A before enrolling. All other courses are open enrollment and not restricted to any grade level.
