Built from the classroom out and co-developed with ICT industry professionals, this framework gives students a real development process — planning, building, reflecting, and iterating across sprint cycles. Students use AI purposefully as a planning tool, not a shortcut. The result is stronger projects, authentic developer habits, and an assessment model that rewards process over product.
