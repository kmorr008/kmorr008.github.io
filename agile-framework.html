Built from the classroom out and co-developed with ICT industry professionals, this framework gives students a real development process — planning, building, reflecting, and iterating across sprint cycles. Students use AI purposefully as a planning tool, not a shortcut. The result is stronger projects, authentic developer habits, and an assessment model that rewards process over product.
What This Framework Does

Solves the Pacing Problem

Sprint cycles build in intermediate accountability. Consistent work isn't just encouraged — it's structurally required.

AI-Proof Assessment

Students are graded on their development process — daily journals, reflections, commits, and feedback. That process has to be lived.

Authentic Industry Practice

Mirrors real agile software development. Students plan before they build, iterate each sprint, and document like developers.
